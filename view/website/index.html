Services

We aim to REUSE, REDUCE, and RECYCLE as many resources as possible. We also continuously ﬁnd the potential new strategies and tactics to convert the waste into something new and valuable to avoid and reduce the trash to dump and harm the environment. As we grew, we started developing recycling partners and tried to offer an all-in-one recycling solution for our clients and meet their needs. We are mainly deals with the collecting of the following waste materials:

Paper Waste

Turning waste paper into new paper products, saving the paper waste to occupy homes of people, and prevent the production of methane from breaking out.

Glass Waste

Glass scraps are considered to be a new source instead of waste. The glass wastes are ﬁrst collected, then after washing them, the scrap items are melted into cullets and then are ﬁnally remoulded into new products.

Plastic Waste

Collecting plastic scraps such as water bottles etc. are ﬁrst sorted by their types then washed and after melting are pelletized and are converted into new products. Water Bottle, Tray sheet converted into cihps or ground form.

e-Waste

Electronic wastes materials such as parts of electrical items that are no longer productive are initially collected, sorted, and later on shredded and are then prepared to be used into some- thing new.
